## Lesser Powers and Places

- **Lanternfall Spire** — a single-industry roost (lamp-crafting; its
  beeswax-and-marrowgas lanterns light half the world) of ~3,000, whose
  windlane is now dying. The campaign's opening crisis.
- **Greywake** — the drowned Spire, jutting from the Undermist at the heart
  of the oldest Stilling. Sovereign territory of nobody; congregation of the
  Hushed.
- **The Spindles** — a column of stripped, sinking spire husks abandoned over
  the centuries, now a vertical shantytown of the roostless. The Crown calls
  it a hazard; ten thousand people call it home.
- **Hollow Vesse** — a dead spire purchased outright by the Marrower Combine
  and converted into the world's only marrow refinery. Armed, private, and
  measurably lower in the sky every year.
- **The lane-shrines** — hundreds of small fixed roosts along every windlane:
  beacon-horn, rain-tank, three days' shelter, and a shrine-cantor who sings
  the lane-songs at dawn. Lane-shrines are neutral ground under the oldest
  law there is. Even Lanhawks honor it. Mostly.
