## Key Locations

- **Suruveil's Crown** — the capital: the largest Spire in the world, visibly
  a titanic horned skull when the light is low, which polite society does not
  mention. Seat of the Spirarchy; home of the Quillate; population ~200,000.
- **The Moult** — the Vael raft-flotilla, currently anchored windward of the
  Crown after abandoning two ancestral windlanes to Stillings.
- **Greywake** — the drowned Spire. Its uppermost hundred feet still jut from
  the Undermist at the heart of the oldest Stilling. The Hushed roost there.
  The Wardens want a survey expedition; nobody sane volunteers.
- **The Windlanes** — the living trade-gales. Lane-shrines along each route
  hold beacon-horns and emergency roosts.
- **The Spire-roots** — the dangling, root-like bone tangles beneath each
  Spire, where marrow-breath seeps and Marrower gangs work in the dark.
