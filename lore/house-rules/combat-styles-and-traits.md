## Combat Style Traits (Veilwrack styles)

Each named style includes its weapons plus one trait:

| Style | Weapons | Trait |
|---|---|---|
| **Warden Skirmisher** | wingspear, stormbow, dagger | *Skirmishing* — may shoot while flying at a Run |
| **Courier's Defense** | dagger, sling-darts, buckler | *Daredevil* — Evade melee attacks without falling prone |
| **Rootworks Scrapper** | talon-hook, buckler, club | *Tunnel Fighter* — no Hard penalty for fighting in confined spaces |
| **Quiet Talons** | natural talons, garrote-cord, dagger | *Knockout Blow* — surprise Stun Location lasts minutes, not turns |
| **Stoopwing** (Ossuin war-rite) | wingspear, talon-hook (2H) | *Stoop Fighter* — diving charge attack penalty is Standard, not Hard |
| **Moult Wall** (Vael militia) | 1H wingspear, kite shield | *Formation Fighting* — per SRD, for shield-walls on raft decks |
