## The Alar

The winged peoples of the Veilwrack call themselves the **Alar**. They are
human-sized avianoids — hollow-boned, keen-eyed, feathered from crown to
tail, with strong arms beneath their wings and clawed, dexterous feet. An
Alar in flight is the master of three dimensions; an Alar grounded is a
person halved. There are three great kindreds:

### The Vael (kestrel-kin)
Swift, slight, restless. The Vael are the couriers, wind-pilots, and traders
of the Veilwrack, living their whole lives along the **windlanes** — the great
trade-gales between Spires. Their nation is **the Moult**, a migrating
flotilla of kite-rafts and tethered gliders thousands strong. They believe
the Breath is a herd, and that it can be *followed* but never owned.
**Culture: Nomadic.** Typical careers: Courier, Wind-pilot, Merchant, Scout.

### The Roak (corvid-kin)
Black-feathered, deliberate, insatiably curious. The Roak are the
memory-keepers, artificers, and archivists. Their guild-city occupies the
upper galleries of Suruveil's Crown, and their great institution, the
**Quillate**, holds the longest unbroken written record in the world —
though even the Quillate's oldest scrolls go strangely silent about the
years before the Crown was settled. The Roak believe the Breath is a
*language*, and that whoever finally parses its grammar will understand
everything. **Culture: Civilized.** Typical careers: Scholar, Crafter,
Official, Physician.

### The Ossuin (condor-kin)
Huge, patient, grave. The Ossuin are the death-priests and Undermist-divers,
the only caste permitted by ancient compact to descend below the Spire-roots.
They carry the dead down to the cloud-sea and return with **mist-pearls** —
condensed droplets of the Undermist that the other kindreds prize and fear in
equal measure. The Ossuin believe the Breath is an *inheritance* — borrowed,
not given — and their oldest funeral chant contains the line that makes Roak
archivists deeply uncomfortable: *"We return what was taken; we do not return
enough."* **Culture: Barbarian.** Typical careers: Priest, Hunter, Physician,
Warrior.

### Alar characteristics (character creation)
Roll as standard Mythras (3d6 / 2d6+6) then apply: **SIZ −3 (min 4), DEX +2**.
Movement: 4m walking, 12m flying. **Flight (STR+DEX)** is a Standard skill;
Swim is not (an Alar in water is in serious trouble). Hit locations use the
avian table (see rules/combat.md). Vael add +5 DEX-based skill of choice;
Roak add +5 INT-based; Ossuin add +5 to Endurance or Willpower.
