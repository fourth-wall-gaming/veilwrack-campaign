## Careers

Use exactly like SRD careers: distribute 100 points among the career's
Standard skills and up to three of its Professional skills.

| Career | Standard Skills | Professional Skills (pick 3) | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Lane Courier** (typically Vael) | Athletics, Endurance, Evade, Flight, Locale, Perception; Combat Style (Courier's Defense) | Acrobatics, Commerce, Culture (any), Language (any), Navigation (Windlanes), Streetwise, Survival | The Moult's lifeblood. Couriers memorize lane-shrines like rosaries. |
| **Gale Warden** | Athletics, Endurance, Evade, First Aid, Flight, Perception; Combat Style (Warden Skirmisher) | Healing, Lore (Stillings), Mechanisms, Navigation (Windlanes), Oratory, Survival, Track | The PCs' default. Lore (Stillings) is the company's hard-won field science. |
| **Quill** (typically Roak) | Customs, Deceit, Influence, Insight, Locale, Native Tongue, Perception | Bureaucracy, Culture (any), Language (any), Literacy, Lore (any two), Research | Archive access is the Quillate's true currency. |
| **Wind-Pilot** | Brawn, Drive (kite-rig), Endurance, Evade, Flight, Perception, Willpower | Boating (raft-handling), Craft (Rigging), Engineering, Mechanisms, Navigation (Windlanes), Lore (Weather) | Flies the Moult's kite-rafts and tow-barges through the lanes. |
| **Death-Diver** (Ossuin only) | Endurance, Flight, Insight, Locale, Perception, Sing, Willpower | Healing, Lore (the Undermist), Lore (Funerary Rites), **Mist-Rites**, Musicianship (chant), Survival | The only legal Undermist descent training. Mist-Rites covers breath-bottles, cold-wax sealing, pressure signs. |
| **Marrow-Miner** | Athletics, Brawn, Conceal, Endurance, Locale, Perception; Combat Style (Rootworks Scrapper) | Commerce, Craft (Gas-bottling), Engineering, Lockpicking, Mechanisms, Navigation (Underground), Streetwise | Knows the spire-roots — and what tapping them does. |
| **Shrine-Cantor** | Customs, Dance, Influence, Insight, Locale, Sing, Willpower | Acting, Courtesy, Lore (Liturgy of the Breath), Musicianship, Oratory, **Windworking**, Teach | Keeps the lane-shrines; many are the Veilwrack's everyday magicians. |
| **Windworker** | Customs, Deceit, Influence, Insight, Locale, Perception, Willpower | Culture (any), Literacy, Lore (the Breath), Lore (Weather), **Windworking**, Oratory, Sleight | The dedicated magician career. See Windworking below. |
| **Crown Agent** | Conceal, Deceit, Evade, Insight, Perception, Stealth; Combat Style (Quiet Talons) | Bureaucracy, Culture (any), Disguise, Language (any), Sleight, Streetwise, Track | The Spirarchy's eyes. Some hunt "still-panic." Some hunt the truth. |
