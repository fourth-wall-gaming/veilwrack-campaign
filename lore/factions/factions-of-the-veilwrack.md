## Factions

| Faction | What they want | What they know |
|---|---|---|
| **The Gale Wardens** | Chart the Stillings, evacuate threatened roosts, find a cause. The PCs' company: part rangers, part rescue service, part heresy in feathers. | Field data the Spirarchy suppresses: the Stillings are accelerating, and they are not random. |
| **The Spirarchy of Suruveil's Crown** | Order, continuity, and the legitimacy of the Crown. | More than they admit. The sealed Tenth Gallery of the palace contains the Founding Compact. |
| **The Quillate** | Knowledge, completeness of the Record. | That the Record has a hole in it exactly one thousand years deep, and that the hole was cut deliberately. |
| **The Hushed Choir** | A cult that worships the Stilling as the world's overdue sabbath. They walk into still zones singing. Some walk back out. | How to survive a Stilling. Nobody has asked them nicely. |
| **The Marrowers** | Profit. They tap and bottle spire-marrow breath as lift-gas for barges and lamps. | That tapped spires sink measurably faster. They have run the numbers. They have burned the numbers. |
