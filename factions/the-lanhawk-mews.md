Independent clutches of 6-20 wings under Talon-captains; the Mews meets each Long Calm to divide lane territories and arbitrate clutch feuds. Negotiable: Lanhawks honor shrine-ground, the bier-right, and ransom custom, and they flog tether-cutters like everyone else. They prey on Combine barges by preference and Crown couriers by necessity. GM: use them as the campaign's recurring, bargainable human-scale threat - and as a desperate ally pool by Act 4, when the lanes everyone shares begin to die.
