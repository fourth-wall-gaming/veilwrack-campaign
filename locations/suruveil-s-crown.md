Seat of the Spirarchy and home of the Quillate archive-guild. Population about 200,000 Alar of all three kindreds. The sealed Tenth Gallery of the palace contains the Founding Compact. Marrower gangs work the spire-roots below; the upper galleries are Roak guild-halls; the Moult is currently anchored windward.
