# Art-Style Card: Engraving

The look of a frontispiece plate in an old book: a single dramatic
black-and-white engraving, printed on foxed rag paper. Used as the preamble for
every per-scene image prompt so the novel's illustrations read as one set.

## Medium
Monochrome ink engraving / etching, in the manner of a 19th-century steel-plate
book illustration or a Gustave Dore frontispiece. Pure black ink on warm
off-white paper. No paint, no photography, no digital gloss -- it should look
printed from an incised plate.

## Palette and value
Black and white only. High-contrast chiaroscuro: deep velvety blacks against
bright untouched paper, a full range of greys built only from line. Light is
dramatic and directional -- lamp-flame, a single break of sky -- and carves the
subject out of darkness.

## Line and texture
Built entirely from engraved line: dense cross-hatching for shadow, fine
parallel burin lines for mid-tone, open paper for highlight, stippling for soft
forms (mist, cloud, smoke). Crisp confident contours. Detail concentrates on the
focal figure and falls away to bold simple masses at the edges.

## Composition
Single full-page plate, portrait or near-square. One clear dramatic subject,
strong foreground silhouette, deep atmospheric recession behind. Generous,
deliberate negative space -- dead air and empty sky read as untouched paper.

## Subject canon
The Veilwrack, a sky-realm of winged folk (player-visible facts only):
- **Alar** are feathered humanoids with real, working wings folded from the
  back -- kestrel-kin (slim, russet-barred), corvid-kin (heavier, black), and
  condor-kin (tall, pale). Not angels, not bird-headed: people with wings.
- **Spires** are the towering bone husks of dead sky-leviathans, hollowed and
  inhabited, hung with rings of small oil-lamps tended on bone walkways.
- Light is **tallow lamp-flame** and thin high daylight. Armour and gear are
  leather harness, bone, and lacquer -- pre-industrial.
- **Dead air / a Stilling** is rendered as flat, dead, untextured space:
  unnaturally still, empty of the mist-grain that fills living sky.

## Mood
Doomed grandeur and quiet dread. Beautiful, perilous, melancholy -- a dying
wind, a small figure against a vast indifferent sky.

## Never do
No colour. No lettering, captions, or signatures inside the image. No modern
objects, no firearms, no machinery. No bird-headed or angelic figures. No
cartoon or anime styling. No GM-only creatures or secrets -- only what the
characters have seen on the page.
