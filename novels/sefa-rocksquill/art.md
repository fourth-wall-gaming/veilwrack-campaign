# Art-Style Card: Comic

Clean modern comic-book interior art. Simple and readable, not fancy. Used as
the look-and-feel reference for a manuscript's illustration prompts. (Prompts
are self-contained — each one restates the style, since the image model never
sees this card.)

## Medium
Hand-drawn modern comic book illustration: bold black ink outlines with clean,
confident, even-weight lines, filled with flat cel-style colour. Not
photorealistic, not painterly, not a 3D/CGI render.

## Palette and value
Full colour but simple — flat colour areas, few gradients, bold local colours,
a slightly muted and grounded palette. Shadows are simple flat shapes and
spotted blacks, not soft rendering.

## Line and texture
Clean even contours, minimal cross-hatching, uncluttered. Readable shapes over
fine detail. Low rendering — keep it simple, not slick.

## Composition
One clear subject, dynamic but easy to read, like a strong comic panel: bold
silhouette, clean separation of foreground and background.

## Subject canon
The Veilwrack, player-visible facts only:
- **Alar** are human-bodied, human-faced people with a real pair of bird wings
  from the back. Kestrel-kin are slim with russet, dark-barred wings; corvid-kin
  heavier and black; condor-kin tall and pale. Never bird-headed, never angels.
- **Spires** are pale bone towers (dead sky-leviathan husks) hung with rings of
  small oil-lamps on bone walkways.
- Gear is plain pre-industrial leather, bone, and harness.

## Mood
Grounded, dynamic, a little gritty. Real people in a strange sky.

## Never do
No painterly or photoreal or 3D/CGI look, no heavy rendering or lens effects.
No engraving/monochrome look. No bird-headed figures or angels. No text,
captions, speech bubbles, logos, or signatures in the image. No modern objects,
firearms, or machinery.
